Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Relationship Minded ("the App") is a personal desktop tool used by its owner to create and publish short video clips to the owner's own TikTok account. This policy explains how data is handled.

2. Data We Access

When you authorize the App with TikTok, the App receives an access token that lets it upload videos to your account. The App accesses only what is required to upload and publish videos via the TikTok Content Posting API.

3. Data Storage

Authorization tokens and generated video files are stored locally on the owner's own computer. The App does not operate a server and does not transmit your data to any third party other than TikTok (for publishing) and YouTube (for sourcing public videos).

4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is exchanged only with TikTok and YouTube APIs as needed to perform the App's functions.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored tokens and files can be deleted at any time by removing them from the computer or by revoking the App's access in your TikTok account settings.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses TikTok and YouTube APIs. Please review their privacy policies for how those services handle data.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. The "last updated" date reflects the latest revision.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the App owner via the project repository.
